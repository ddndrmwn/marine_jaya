IC-M94D AIS Traffic Info, Now in Your Hand

Icom’s M94D is a Class-H DSC VHF transceiver and is the world’s first handheld to possess the power of AIS. A class-leading 1500 mW speaker and ocean-conscious features, the M94D is perfect for small boats and leisure craft looking to stay safe and informed on the ocean.

GENERAL

  • Frequency Tx: 156.025–157.425 MHz
  • Frequency Rx: 156.050–163.275 MHz
  • Frequency Rx (CH70): 156.525 MHz
  • Frequency Rx (AIS): 162.025 MHz
  • Usable channel groups: INT, USA, CAN, WX channels
  • Type of emission: 16K0G3E (FM), 16K0G2B (DSC), 16K0GXW (GMSK)
  • Current drain (at 7.2 V DC) TX (6 W/1 W output): 1500 mA/700 mA (approx)
  • Current drain (at 7.2 V DC) RX (Inter SP/Exter SP Max. audio): 700 mA/350 mA (approx)
  • Operating temperature range: –20°C to +60°C; –4˚F to +140˚F
  • Antenna impedance: 50 Ω
  • Dimensions (W×H×D) (Projections not included): 60.9 × 145.8 × 43.8 mm; 2.4 × 5.7 × 1.7 in
  • Weight (approximately): 357 g ; 12.6 oz (With BP-306, antenna, MB-133)
