IC-M804 Long Range Communications Beyond the Horizon

The IC-M804 meets ITU-R M.493-15 and ETSI EN 300 338-4 Class E DSC regulations. In an emergency, by pressing the large independent Distress button, a digital Distress signal is sent with GNSS coordinates and calls for help to other ships or coast stations.

GENERAL

  • Frequency coverage receive: 500 kHz–29.9999 MHz (continuous)
  • Frequency coverage transmit: 1.6–2.9999, 4.0–4.9999, 6.0–6.9999, 8.0–8.9999, 12.0–13.9999, 16.0–17.9999, 18.0–19.9999, 22.0–22.9999, 25.0–27.5000 MHz
  • Frequency coverage DSC: 2.1875, 4.2075, 6.3120, 8.4145, 12.5770, 16.8045 MHz
  • Mode Tx/Rx: J3E (USB), J2B (AFSK), F1B (FSK)
  • Mode Rx Only: J3E (LSB), A1A (CW), H3E (AM)
  • Mode DSC: F1B
  • Antenna impedance: 50 Ω (unbalanced)
  • Usable temperature range: –15 ℃ to 55 ℃
  • Frequency stability Tx/Rx: ±10 Hz (below 29.9999 MHz)
  • Frequency stability DSC: ±10 Hz
  • Power supply requirement: 10.8–15.6 V (12 V DC), 21.6–31.2 V (24 V DC) (negative ground)
  • Current drain receive: Less than 6 A (12 V) / 3A (24 V)(Maximum audio output)
  • Current drain transmit: Less than 40 A (12 V)/20 A (24 V) (Max. output power) (with 1.1 kHz and 1.7 kHz AF input)
  • Dimensions (Projections not included) main unit: 367 (W) × 95 (H) × 260 (D) mm
  • Dimensions (Projections not included) controller: 274 (W) × 114 (H) × 86 (D) mm
  • Weight main unit: 8.6 kg (approximate)
  • Weight controller: 760 g (approximate)

TRANSMITTER

  • Output power 1.6-3.9999 MHz: 85 W pep
  • Output power 4.0-27.5000 MHz: 125 W pep
  • Spurious emissions: Less than -50 dB peak output power (at Max. Power)
  • Carrier suppression: More than 40 dB peak output power (at Max. Power)
  • Unwanted sideband suppression: More than 55 dB peak output power (at Max power with 1500 Hz AF input)

RECEIVER

  • Sensitivity J3E A1A 0.5–1.5999 MHz: 30 dBμV emf (20 dB SINAD)
  • Sensitivity J2B F1B 1.6–29.9999 MHz: 0 dBμV emf (1% error rate)
  • Sensitivity H3E 0.5–1.5999 MHz: 44 dBμV emf (20 dB SINAD)
  • Sensitivity H3E 1.6–3.9999 MHz: 24 dBμV emf (20 dB SINAD)
  • DSC Sensitivity: 0 dBμV emf (1% error rate)
  • Squelch sensitivity J3E (at 12.230 MHz) (Threshold): Less than 26 dBμV emf
  • Squelch sensitivity J3E (at 12.230 MHz) (Tight): Less than 96 dBμV emf
  • Squelch sensitivity H3E (at 1.000 MHz) (Threshold): Less than 36 dBμV emf
  • Squelch sensitivity H3E (at 1.000 MHz) (Tight): Less than 116 dBμV emf
  • Audio output power: 4.0 W at 10% distortion with 4 Ω load
  • Clarity variable range: ±150 Hz
