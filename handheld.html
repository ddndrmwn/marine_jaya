Great features and style are the traits of Icom’s M25 floating marine VHF radio, which floats and flashes when dropped into the water. Sporting a slim design, this handheld has the lightest body in the market for a floating VHF radio. New for an Icom marine handheld, this radio requires a USB connector for convenient charging from a variety of electronic devices.

Designed for commercial and recreational boaters, the M37 comes with 6W of power for further communications range. The M37 includes all the great features in the M36, plus Icom’s exclusive Float’n Flash and a larger capacity standard battery.

The M73’s ergonomic design fits easily in the user’s hand and is a rugged, durable radio. The large screen and high-intensity LCD offer bright and easy-to-read characters. Yacht customers benefit from the optional VOX headset and adapter option that allows someone handling dock lines to use both hands while docking the boat, simultaneously communicating with the captain.

The M85 is the smallest, lightest, and toughest radio on the market today! The M85 is built to MIL-STD 810 and can withstand hazardous and inhospitable environments, both on land and at sea. The IP67 waterproof rating protects the radio even if dropped in water. Yacht and commercial customers like the M85 because of the 100 programmable land mobile channels, as well as the easy-to-use built-in voice scrambler for private communications. The M85 is full of the features you need, including an automated emergency alarm.

Icom’s M94D is a Class-H DSC VHF transceiver and is the world’s first handheld to possess the power of AIS. A class-leading 1500 mW speaker and ocean-conscious features, the M94D is perfect for small boats and leisure craft looking to stay safe and informed on the ocean.

The GM1600 Marine VHF handheld is designed for GMDSS compliant survival craft communications. The radio is engineered to survive and function in the extreme environmental conditions common in marine emergencies and brings the latest Search and Rescue technology to the marketplace. The GM1600’s shock-resistant housing is constructed of a brightly colored dye-injected polymer assuring this essential piece of emergency equipment will always be easy to find in a survival bag, in a life raft, or any other place when you need to find it quickly. Survival Boat Package (GM1600): Includes dated-and-sealed GMDSS compliant battery (BP-234).
